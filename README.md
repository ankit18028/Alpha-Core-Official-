Alpha Core eSports is a growing platform designed for players who want to improve their skills, compete with others, and be part of a disciplined and active gaming environment. Our goal is to provide fair competition, regular practice opportunities, and a strong community experience.

- Daily & weekly scrims
- Organized Discord server with active moderation
- Fair gameplay and structured matches
- Opportunities to grow as a competitive player
